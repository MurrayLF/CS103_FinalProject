General Info

Dolphins are aquatic mammals found throughout the world's oceans. Different species and individuals range in size from 5-to-31 feet in length. Some dolphins have been found to reach 18 mph and be capable of leaping 30 feet in the air.

Dolphin Calls

Dolphins are well known for their clicks and squeaks. In actuality, dolphins do not possess vocal cords, instead relying on muscles around their blowhole to produce sound.
