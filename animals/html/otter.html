General Info

Otters are carnivorous mammals in the subfamily Lutrinae. The 13 extant otter species are all semiaquatic, aquatic or marine, with diets based on fish and invertebrates. They are also closely related to weasels, badgers, and wolverines.

Common Otter Misconceptions

Otters are neither nocturnal or dinural (awake during only the daytime) but rather they are active during bot the day and the night and take short naps throughout the day for rest. It is also commonly believed that otters only eat fish andsometimes kill more than they eat making them the enemy of fisherman. This is untrue, because otters eat both fish and other animals and generally only kill fish which they intend to eat.
