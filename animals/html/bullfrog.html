General Info

Bullfrogs are native to North America, but can now be found across the Americas, Europe, and East Asia. Like other true frogs, bullfrogs hatch from eggs as tadpoles, before developing into mature frogs.

Bullfrog Calls

Bullfrogs received their name from the croak they make, which is said to resemble a bull's grunt. The presence of these amphibians is often only revealed by their nocturnal choruses.
