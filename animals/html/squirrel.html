General Info

Squirrels are rodents which also makes them mammals. There are over 200 Species of squirrels across the globe. The Eastern Grey Squirrel generally ranges in size from 400g-600g. Squirrels are not rabies vector species but they can carry other infectious diseases like lyme disease.

Squirrel Agility

Squirrels are extremely agile creatures which makes sense given that most spend their lives climbing through the trees. Squirrels are able to surive large falls because they always land on their feet and have so little mass that the imapct of landing doesnt hurt them. Squirrels are also quite clever which makes them perfect for running through an obstacle course.

Common Squirrel Misconceptions

Flying squirrels are one of the most popular types of squirels even though they dont actually fly, instead they glide from branch to branch. Another common misconception about squirrels is that they all live in the tree tops, in reality squirrels live in trees as well as burrows in the ground. Hibernation is also much less common among squirrel species with only ground dwelling squirrels hibernating regularly. Black squirrels, more common in the western US, are not a disitinct species from brown squirrels, but rather have different genes for coat color.
